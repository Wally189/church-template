Welcome to the parish

A harbour-side church for worship, prayer, and neighbourly life.

St. Anselm by the Sea is a parish church in Whitstable serving long-time residents, new arrivals, families, and visitors looking for a thoughtful and prayerful Christian community on the north Kent coast.

Service times Parish life
What we do Sung Eucharist, daily prayer, baptisms, weddings, funerals, pastoral care, and regular parish events.
Who it is for Local households, children and families, older parishioners, newcomers to faith, and visitors to the coast.

This week at St. Anselm's

Prayerful, steady, and open through the week

Sunday worship is anchored by the 10.30am Sung Eucharist, with weekday Morning Prayer, a Wednesday midweek Communion, and quiet pastoral appointments arranged through the parish office.

Parish life

  • Children's table and family corner available every Sunday.
  • Monthly parish lunch and Thursday community pantry.
  • Hall hire for local groups, rehearsals, and daytime meetings.
  • Home communion and visiting for those unable to attend in person.

Serving Whitstable with worship, prayer, and practical care through the week.
