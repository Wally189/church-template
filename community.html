Parish life

Shared meals, neighbourly support, and practical care beyond Sunday.

St. Anselm's is active throughout the week with children's activities, community meals, pastoral visiting, and room hire for local groups that need a calm and dependable meeting place.

Regular activities

Thursday Pantry 11.00am to 1.00pm with tea, food support, and local signposting.
Little Anselm's Friday 9.30am toddler morning with crafts, songs, and coffee.
Parish Lunch First Sunday of the month in the church hall after the main service.

Practical support

  • Pastoral visiting for housebound parishioners.
  • Bereavement follow-up and memorial planning.
  • Hall bookings for choirs, community groups, and rehearsals.
  • School links and occasional civic services.
